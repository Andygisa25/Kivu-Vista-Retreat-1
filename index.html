Our Story

A retreat built
for real rest

Kivu Vista Retreat was born from a simple belief: that great hospitality doesn't need to be loud. Set near the calming shores of Lake Kivu in Rwanda, our property offers a refined escape for travellers who value quiet comfort, natural beauty, and genuine care.

With 14 thoughtfully designed rooms, we blend modern minimalism with the warmth of Rwandan hospitality — creating a space where every detail has been considered and every guest feels at home.
